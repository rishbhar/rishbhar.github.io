  Work Experience

User Experience Design Intern - Musejam


Role

Sketching, Wireframing, User Activity Analytics


Tools

Photoshop CC, Sketch

Duration

3 months

I wanted to do some professional work specifically in user experience design before starting my Masters in HCI. I joined Musejam, a startup whose vision is to bring the Indian Live Music Industry together. The website lists all the live events and artists from India. I had a great time working with them because I got to wireframe a lot. Also, I got the chance to work with a complete team in a startup. I was involved in decision making on design elements and communication with users. I designed new screens and proposed improvements for the website. Besides work, discovering new music every week was just too sweet.
