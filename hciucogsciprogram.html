Indiana University Cognitive Science Program
The Cognitive Science Program at Indiana University is dedicated to the study of cognition and information. Cognitive science is the interdisciplinary study of mind and the nature of intelligence. The program brings together faculty and students from many different departments, and it encourages integrated and cooperative research between all participating disciplines. Because of the many labs that are affiliated with the program, undergraduate and graduate students receive unique teaching and hands-on research experiences (http://cogs.indiana.edu/about-us/affiliates.php).
